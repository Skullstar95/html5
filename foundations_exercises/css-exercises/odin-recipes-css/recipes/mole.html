Mole

Mole is a traditional Mexican sauce made with a variety of ingredients, including chili peppers, chocolate, and spices. It is often served over chicken or turkey and is known for its rich and complex flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 dried ancho chiles
  • 2 dried pasilla chiles
  • 2 dried mulato chiles
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup Mexican chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Remove the stems and seeds from the dried chiles. Toast them in a dry skillet over medium heat for 1-2 minutes, or until fragrant. Transfer to a bowl and cover with hot water. Let soak for 20 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, toast the sesame seeds and almonds until golden brown. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In a blender, combine the soaked chiles, toasted sesame seeds, almonds, raisins, onion, garlic, tomatoes, chicken broth, chocolate, cinnamon, cloves, black pepper, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour the mole sauce into a saucepan and cook over medium heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened. Serve over cooked chicken or turkey.
